  • 1. Accelerate Category penetration amongst Spirited Strivers by delivering incontinent solutions at each incontinence level with Comfort, Discretion & Design that restores normalcy and gives the confidence to engage:

    • a. Renovate briefs portfolio by enhancing product experience for the caregiver (Vampiro II).
    • b. Establish a multi-tier portfolio strategy by region.
    • c. Develop powerful claims for Plenitud Protect & Classic.
    • d. Launch HIP to accelerate migration from brief to pants.
  • 3. Increase Wipes Brand penetration with Caregivers of inactive adults by delivering improved consumer experience.
    • a. Renovate our current Wipe ( from 40’s to 50’s count with an improve dispenser system).
    • b. Develop a low count for promotional activities with Brief (1 brief – 1 Wipe).
    • c. Explore Coform solution for a premium AC wipe solution.
  • 4. Sustain current margins growth rates by combining accelerated topline growth, pricing and cost efficiency.

Fundamentals

Brand Story

Life is a constant change. The years go by, we grow and we mature. The years of maturity are to be taken advantage of. We have more experience, more knowledge and more wisdom, and we have more time to enjoy our loved ones.

We also know that as we get older we start experiencing certain physical limitations, which may affect our dignity and confidence, preventing us to carry out certain things we wish to do.

Plenitud helps us deal with these changes in a positive way, because we know that active adults need to continue feeling secure, so that nothing changes their way of life.

Plenitud offers innovative solutions beyond those currently found in the market: each products is carefully developed; and every detail is thought out so that the elder will continue to naturally lead an active life, to enjoy life at it fullest.

Being premium products, Plenitud offers the protection, comfort and discretion we need to feel more confident in any situation.
